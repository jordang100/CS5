Hacking Google's Dinosaur Game (Anti-Collision)
Step 1 Disconnect from the internet and open up chrome. You will be greeted with the Google dinosaur game.
Step 2 Right click and select inspect element. From there, click on console and paste in the following code:
Runner.prototype.gameOver = function() {console.log("hi")}
Step 3 Press enter and close the console. Press space to begin the game and notice how your dinosaur never dies :)


Hacking Google's Dinosaur Game (Speed Boost)
Step 1 Disconnect from the internet and open up chrome. You will be greeted with the Google dinosaur game.
Step 2 Right click and select inspect element. From there, click on console and paste in the following code:
Runner.instance_.setSpeed(1000);
Step 3 Press enter and close the console. Note that you can change the number to alter the dinosaur's speed. Press space to begin the game.
